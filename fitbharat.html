    🥗 Indian Diet Tips

    Balanced eating rooted in Indian traditions — seasonal produce, whole grains, lentils and mindful eating.

    • Start with warm water and soaked nuts in the morning.
    • Include dal/legumes and a vegetable with each meal.
    • Prefer millets and whole grains; reduce refined sugar and fried snacks.
    • Include curd or buttermilk for gut health.
